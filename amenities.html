LABORATORY

Class room teaching and computer laboratory are supplemented with exposure to information and training material available on CD-ROMs and through Internet. The emphasis is on using the main application laboratory as the major focus of teaching and training programme. This enables students to acquire capabilities of self-learning, critical and analytical thinking not only to cope with existing software packages and languages but encourages them to design new packages. The main application laboratory already consists of Apple X-Server, Acer & Apple I-Mac and SUN Enterprise 450 edition Servers. Students are allowed to work on heterogeneous operating environments. Separate new Linux lab & Apple Mac Lab is also in place. The facility of latest multimedia systems, digital cameras, Multimedia projector, CD- server are in the main application laboratory for Interactive Computer Based Teaching (CBT). A separate Linux workstation lab is also in place. The Networking laboratory with real time experimental facilities is one of the major feature. The Hardware (digital, microprocessor and communication) laboratory is also in place for in-depth exposure to students.
